BEEP's Adventure
YouTube | Download | WebGL
BEEP's Adventure is a project I worked on through both semesters of my final year.

I have hosted the game on WebGL that you can play here.

I was a programmer in a group of 7 with two designers, two animators, one other programmer, and a producer.

I worked on this project with documented 14+ hours of work each week. This game gave me the experience of creating a large-scale indie game; going through each phase of production to create a polished, user-tested, and publishable game.
Mech AI Tournament Champion
My "AI for Games" class of Semester 1 2020 ran a Mech AI tournament that I won. Its livestream can be found here.

This tournament involved using behaviour trees via PandaBT in Unity to run in-script behaviours programmed in C#. We were only able to change the robot's decisions, not their physical abilities. Our behaviour scripts were then attached to Mech prefabs and pitched against each other in a round-based tournament.

There were 24 participants from our class, and I made it to the semi-finals, then to the finals, and won the finals. My AI bot also beat the top 3 champions from 2019 in a bonus round.
